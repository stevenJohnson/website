About

I am a second year PhD Student studying Human-Computer Interaction at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I am working with my advisor, Bilge Mutlu, on projects exploring the effectiveness of a variety of technologies in supporting collaborative and educational outcomes.

I am also a NASA Space Technology Research Fellowship (NSTRF) recipient. Currently, I am developing a head-mounted display-based task guidance system to study how these systems can support crew members engaged in physical tasks, such as during maintenance and repair procedures.

My current research interests include computer-mediated communication, wearable and mobile computing, and educational technologies.
