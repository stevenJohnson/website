About

I am a first year PhD Student studying Human-Computer Interaction at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I am a research assistant working with my advisor, Bilge Mutlu, on projects exploring the effectiveness of a variety of technologies in supporting collaborative and educational outcomes.

My current research interests include computer-mediated communication, wearable and mobile computing, and educational technologies.
